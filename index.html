Hydrogen formation quench

Classical real-time simulation of bosonized 1+1D two-flavour QED. The fields are \(\phi_c=(\phi_1+\phi_2)/\sqrt{2}\) and \(\phi_s=(\phi_1-\phi_2)/\sqrt{2}\). This version uses nontrivial spatial initial profiles and sets all initial momenta to zero.

For non-periodic boundary conditions one may choose an integer spin-sector charge \(z\), initialized by \(\phi_s(-L/2,0)=0\), \(\phi_s(L/2,0)=z\sqrt{2\pi}\), and \(\phi_c(\pm L/2,0)=0\). For \(z=0\), the profile is a central flavour-1 kink--antikink pair with \(\phi_2=0\).

Dashed lines mark topological sectors. For \(\phi_c,\phi_s\): \(n\sqrt{2\pi}\). For \(\phi_1,\phi_2\): \(n\sqrt{\pi}\).
